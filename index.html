INTRODUCTION

At Celio Entertainment, we create narratives that are bold, character-driven, and based on universal human dilemmas and South Africa's dynamic essence. Belgrado, our main project, is a gritty family drama that explores power, self-love, and retribution. It combines very intimate feeling with cinematic narrative.

Belgrado isn’t just a show — it’s an experience. A layered world of love, betrayal, and survival, grounded in the streets, boardrooms, and hidden corners of modern South Africa. Our goal is to give fans something real: unforgettable characters, raw moments, and a story that feels both intimate and epic.

For Partners & Streamers: Belgrado is designed for long-form storytelling with multi-season potential, appealing to both local and global audiences. With unique cultural depth and international narrative hooks, it bridges untapped African narratives with the cinematic scale and structure of premium TV. We’re building not just a series, but a franchise-ready IP with cross-media potential (music, games, spin-offs).
